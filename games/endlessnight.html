The Endless Night

Roles: Designer, Programmer

Platform: Windows PC, Wii U

Engine: Custom

Language: Lua

Duration: 1 year

Team Size: 2


  • Designed systems for player-world interaction inspired by point and click games like Day of the Tentacle and Full Throttle.
  • Created custom text parse system for reading player input and item collection. Wrote tools for easy dialogue tree editing and cutscene creation for non-programmers.
  • Designed characters, their backstory in the world, and general narrative for main and "side" objectives in game. Drew heavy inspiration from tales of the American Southwest and 20th century Cryptozoology.
  • Programmed adventure game system with inventory, quest tracking, player movement, dialogue trees, and world stat tracking originally in Lua. Currently porting to C++.
