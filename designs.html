Counter Strike Global Offensive Custom Mission: "Retribution"

March 16, 2018

In December 2017, I began playing around with the idea of creating a custom mission for CS:GO. The idea came from the realization that Counter Strike hasn't had singleplayer content since...2001? The CSGO distro of Source still very much can handle it, as seen with the Training Course tutorial map made for players.

I pulled together a small team of myself, another mapper, and another scripter to work on this project. We set our sights on a working demo before GDC 2018 and hit the mark. Lots of scripted events, custom little gameplay tweaks, and tools made allowed us to make a somewhat functional singleplayer map for CSGO. We had a great time, and got some really good feedback at the conference and during GDC. Will definitely come back to finish the project when we're all free again :).
