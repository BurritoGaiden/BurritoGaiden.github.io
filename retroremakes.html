Retro Remakes

Role: Game Designer, Programmer Platform: Windows PC Engine: Unity Game Engine Language: C# Duration: 2 days Team Size: 1

Detailed Information: Retro Remakes is how I express my love for games from the past that have influenced gaming today. I greatly enjoy taking old games and remaking them in new engines to see what I am capable of. But I also do it to try to get a better understanding of the simple mechanics that made these individual games classics. Someday I'll take my remakes and put entertaining twists on them to give the games new life. So far I've remade Pong and Tetris. Pong?!? is like your standard fair pong but instead of a points system and the ball resetting after an unsuccesful return each player has hitpoints. Players gain hit points for successfully returning the ball and lose hit points for letting it hit their wall. The game speeds up as time goes on so after a while losing and gaining points becomes a life or death battle. Tetris?!? is a version of tetris made specifically for my monitor. The game appears dark but late at night appears perfectly visible on my computer. If it isn't playable on your machine then you might need to turn up the gamma/brightness. Might add more features but for now it's a simple tetris clone for those with sensitive eyes? Pong?!: Ironic that the ?!? is there. I don't remember where it is. I'll reupload when found.
