Process

As a game designer, I've done a lot of different work, in various roles.


I've found that different articles, resources, guides have helped me greatly when taking on new aspects of game design.


Level Design

I like to think I'm old fashioned in terms of how I approach Level Design. I first think of the space. What general settings are available in the game I'm designing for? What kind of impact does this level have on the narrative of the game? What kind of props and level geometry need to be placed where to best compliment the gameplay mechanisms, player interactions, and plot events.
