Heroes Rift

Roles: Designer

Platform: Tabletop

Engine: Paper

Language: English

Duration: 5 months

Team Size: 6


Heroes Rift is a card game developed as the Semester project for GADIG in the Fall of 2014. Our focus was on varied gameplay for each match so we created three different decks with different playstyles for each that players can choose from.
